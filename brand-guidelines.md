# Vintage Travel - Brand Design System (v1.0)
**Source:** Brand Development Document (Sept 2025)
**Tagline:** "The art of the Mediterranean."
**Core Concept:** Authentic character, warmth, sunwarmed terracotta, and the true spirit of the region.

## 1. Color Palette: "Rooted in the Mediterranean"
The palette reflects the earth, stone, and flora of the region.
* **Primary Brand Colors:**
    * **Terracotta:** `#C06C54` (Warm, earthy rust/orange - Primary Brand Color)
    * **Olive:** `#5F6B4E` (Deep, desaturated green - Secondary/Text)
* **Neutral / Backgrounds:**
    * **Clay:** `#E8E4DD` (Soft warm beige/off-white - Page Backgrounds)
    * **Stone:** `#A8A29E` (Warm grey - Borders/Secondary Text)
* **Accents:**
    * **Palm:** `#4A7A45` (Vibrant living green - Success/Highlights)
    * **Soleil:** `#D4A017` (Sun yellow - Accents)
    * **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Negative space)

## 2. Typography: "A Timeless Pairing"
* **Headings (Serif):** `Crimson Pro`
    * **Weights:** Light (300) for large display, Medium (500) for subheads.
    * **Usage:** Main headlines, hero text, the "VINTAGE TRAVEL" wordmark.
    * **Style:** Elegant, editorial, "Old World" feel.
* **Body (Sans-Serif):** `Inter`
    * **Weights:** Regular (400), SemiBold (600).
    * **Usage:** UI elements, navigation, pricing, long-form reading.
    * **Style:** Clean, modern utility.

## 3. Logo & Iconography
* **The Mark:** "The Palm" - A stylized line-drawing combining a palm frond and the letter 'T'.
* **Wordmark:** "VINTAGE TRAVEL" in Crimson Pro capitals.
* **Motifs:** Tile-inspired idents (geometric, floral, mosaic patterns).

## 4. UI & Layout Principles
* **Mosaic Grids:** Use "1-up, 2-down" image grids.
* **Imagery:** High-resolution, texture-heavy (terracotta tiles, olive groves, pools).
* **Whitespace:** Generous padding. Minimal clutter.
* **Cards:** 3:2 aspect ratio for villa cards. Minimal text overlay.
